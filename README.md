# SaaS Homepage Conversion Checklist

A practical, zero-fluff checklist for early-stage B2B SaaS founders reviewing a homepage before spending more on traffic.

## Five-second clarity

- [ ] The hero names a specific target customer.
- [ ] The headline promises a concrete, valuable outcome.
- [ ] A visitor can explain what the product does after five seconds.
- [ ] The primary CTA is visually dominant and states what happens next.

## Trust and proof

- [ ] Meaningful customer proof appears before the first major scroll.
- [ ] Product screenshots or real outputs show what the buyer receives.
- [ ] Claims are specific and supportable.
- [ ] The page answers the buyer's biggest objection.

## Buying momentum

- [ ] Pricing or the buying process is clear.
- [ ] The page gives hesitant visitors a lower-commitment next step.
- [ ] Every section moves the visitor toward one conversion goal.
- [ ] Mobile visitors can understand and act without friction.
